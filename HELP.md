# User Guide how to use web-app SB_Sagyz

## Introduction

Welcome to the SB_Sagyz web-app! This guide can help you start work with our application and explain all main function in it.

## Registration and Login

1. **Registration**:

   - Go to the registration page at `/register`.
   - Fill out the registration form with your email, username, and password.
   - Click the "Register" button.
2. **Login**:

   - Go to the login page at `/login`.
   - Enter your email and password.
   - Click the "Login" button.

## User Dashboard

After signing in, you will be redirected to your dashboard where you can:

- View your personal details.
- View your query history.

## Scanning

1. Open the scanning page at `/create_query`.
2. Enter the server IP or domain you want to scan.
3. Click the "Start Scan" button.
4. Wait for the scan to finish and review the results.

## Viewing Vulnerabilities

1. Open the vulnerabilities page at `/vulnerabilities`.
2. Browse the list of vulnerabilities found in the system.

## Logout

To sign out, click the "Logout" button in the top menu.
